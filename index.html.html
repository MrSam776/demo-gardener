What I do

Everything your garden needs

From a weekly lawn cut to a full seasonal clear-out, I bring the right tools, the right knowledge, and I always leave the garden — and the street — exactly as I found them.

01
Lawn Care
Regular cutting and precise edging to keep your lawn looking sharp throughout the season. I work to the shape of your garden, not a standard template.
02
Hedge Trimming
Neat, even trimming to keep hedges tidy and well-shaped. Cuts made at the right time of year to encourage healthy growth.
03
Tree Maintenance
Pruning and safe removal of dead branches. PA1 & PA6 licensed where pesticide or specialist treatment is involved.
04
Planting
New flowers and plants chosen and positioned to bring colour, structure, and life to your garden all year round.
05
Weeding
Thorough clearance of flower beds and borders so your plants can thrive without competition.
06
Waste Removal
All garden waste taken away at the end of every visit. Nothing left on the street, nothing left in your bins.

About me

Local, reliable, and genuinely careful with your garden

I'm James, a Cambridgeshire-based gardener with a genuine passion for outdoor spaces. I take on private garden maintenance work alongside my full-time role — which means I keep my client list manageable so every garden gets the attention it deserves.

I come fully equipped with my own tools, I'm PA1 and PA6 licensed, and I always take everything away with me. Most of my work comes through word of mouth from happy customers — the best sign I know that I'm getting it right.
